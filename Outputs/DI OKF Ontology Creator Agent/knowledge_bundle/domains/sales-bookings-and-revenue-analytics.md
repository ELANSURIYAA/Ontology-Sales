# Sales Bookings and Revenue Analytics

## Description

Supports analysis of completed sales bookings for enterprise products and subscription services across customers, products, partners, geographies, sales representatives, contracts, and fiscal periods.

## Business Purpose

This domain enables commercial performance analysis of booked business. It supports revenue-oriented analytics, customer and product portfolio insight, channel and partner analysis, geographic performance analysis, seller performance tracking, contract value analysis, and period-based reporting.
